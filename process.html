Investment workflow

Institutional process from research to portfolio action

AE follows a disciplined pipeline where each recommendation must survive deep scrutiny before any capital is deployed.

  1. 01

    Sector screening

    Coverage teams monitor market structure, competitive shifts, and valuation dislocations.

  2. 02

    Deep diligence

    Analysts build models, examine catalysts, and map downside pathways with explicit assumptions.

  3. 03

    Debate and review

    Senior members pressure-test thesis quality, evidence strength, and risk controls.

  4. 04

    Portfolio inclusion

    Only highest-conviction case studies are approved for live capital allocation.
