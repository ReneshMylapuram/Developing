Who we are

Building the next generation of disciplined investors

Advanced Equities is a student-run investment and research organization focused on long-term analytical skill-building, high-quality decision processes, and real capital stewardship.

Mission

Developing research-driven investors with institutional habits

AE trains students to think like professionals: build a thesis from first principles, stress-test assumptions, communicate risk clearly, and make decisions with discipline. Our learning model combines mentorship, technical depth, and accountability to portfolio outcomes.

Research Rigor

Institutional-style reports, valuation frameworks, and evidence-based recommendations.

Merit-Driven Growth

Responsibility and influence increase with demonstrated quality and conviction.
